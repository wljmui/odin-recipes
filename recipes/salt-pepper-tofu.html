Crispy Salt & Pepper Tofu

Description

A Chinese takeaway classic - but DIY.

Ingredients

Serves 4:

  • 5 spring onions
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 9 bird's eye chillies
  • 560g firm tofu
  • 6 tbsp cornflour
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • salt
  • pepper
  • vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Add pepper, salt, and sugar in a bowl. Mix it.
  2. Slice tofu into cubes. Add cornflour to bowl and coat the tofu in it.
  3. Fry the tofu in the vegetable oil until crispy on all sides (large frying pan, medium-high heat).
  4. Finely chop spring onions, garlic, chillies. Fry them off and add tofu to combine.
  5. Sprinkle pepper, salt, and sugar mixture over tofu and stir through.
