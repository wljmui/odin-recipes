Tamago Kake Gohan

One of my favourite quick, comforting meals to prepare.

Ingredients

Serves 1:

  • 1 cup hot cooked white rice
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon soy sauce
  • kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon mirin (optional)
  • MSG (optional)
  • furikake, to taste

Steps

  1. Place rice in bowl and, in a shallow indent, break a whole egg. Season with soy sauce, mirin, and MSG.
  2. Use chopsticks to mix it all together; it should end up being quite frothy and fluffy.
  3. Sprinkle furikake and adjust seasonings as necessary. Serve immediately.
