Japanese Cold Brew Coffee

Simply put, it's coffee brewed hot onto ice.

Ingredients

Makes roughly 500ml:

  • 225g ice
  • 30g medium-coarse ground coffee
  • 225g near-boiling water

Steps

  1. Set a carafe with ice on scales.
  2. Set up V60 with paper filter and add ground coffee.
  3. Bloom coffee for 30s.
  4. Pour rest of the water all over grounds.
  5. Allow water to fully drain through filter. Then, discard grounds.
